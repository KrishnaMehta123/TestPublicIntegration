## Overview

This section covers how to delete a role. 


#Delete a Role
To delete a role, perform the following steps:
1. From the dashboard, navigate to *Settings* > *Roles*.
2. Click on the vertical ellipsis of the corresponding role. 
3. Click on the trash can icon.
