## Overview

This section covers how to edit a role.

# Edit a Role

To edit a role, perform the following steps:

1. From the dashboard, navigate to *Settings* > *Roles*.
2. Click on the vertical ellipsis of the corresponding role. 
3. Click the pencil icon.

![1401](https://files.readme.io/d6c5c51-Edit_a_role_1.png "Edit a role 1.png")

4. On the new window popup, click **Proceed**.

![471](https://files.readme.io/d553998-Edit_a_role_2.png "Edit a role 2.png")

5. Review the component access, then click **Save & Continue**.

![1401](https://files.readme.io/80820df-Edit_a_role_3.png "Edit a role 3.png")

6. Select whether the role can access data for all users or selected users.
7. Click **Save & Continue**. 

![1411](https://files.readme.io/f18c02d-Edit_a_role_4.png "Edit a role 4.png")

8. Review the edited role, then click **Edit Role**.

![1406](https://files.readme.io/d64c770-Edit_a_role_5.png "Edit a role 5.png")

9. Enter a role name.
10. Click **Save**.

![480](https://files.readme.io/66491fd-Edit_a_role_6.png "Edit a role 6.png")
