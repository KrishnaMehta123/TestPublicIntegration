## Overview

This section covers how to clone a role.

# Clone a Role

To clone a role, perform the following steps:

1. From the dashboard, navigate to _Settings_ > _Roles_.
2. Click on the vertical ellipsis of the corresponding role. 
3. Click on the collated page icon.

4. Review the component access, then click **Save & Continue**.

![](https://files.readme.io/cfb145c-Clone_a_role_2.png "Clone a role 2.png")

5. Select whether this role can access data for all users or selected users.
6. Click **Save & Continue**. 

![](https://files.readme.io/6786074-Clone_a_role_3.png "Clone a role 3.png")

7. Review the cloned role, then click **Create Role**.

![](https://files.readme.io/873af12-Clone_a_role_4.png "Clone a role 4.png")

8. Enter a role name.
9. Click **Save**.

![](https://files.readme.io/0aa6c01-Clone_a_role_5.png "Clone a role 5.png")
