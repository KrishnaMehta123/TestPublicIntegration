CleverTap for Startups is running a special offer under the Black Friday Sale. You can enjoy the CleverTap Essential Plan with 5000 MAUs for free during the first six months of registration. After this initial period, the plan typically costs $75 per month (₹6000 INR + Taxes). To register, visit the [Sign Up ](https://clevertap.com/startup-pricing/) page.

The following are the terms and conditions to avail the offer:

* Offer is valid only for the 5000 MAU tier of the CleverTap Essentials plan.
* The free period of six months includes the free trial period of one month.
* Offer is applicable for both the base plan and add-ons.
* Offer is valid from 15 November 2023 through 30 November 2023.
* Offer is applicable only to new CleverTap accounts.
* Offer is removed if the billable MAU crosses 5000 MAU in any month within the six-month offer period.
* Offer is removed during account cancellation, migration, or reactivation.
* The free period is applicable for the first six months. After six months, you are billed $75/month (₹6000 INR + Taxes) for 5000 MAUs or as per the selected plan pricing.
* Offer cannot be combined with any other offer, goal-based incentive, or promotional discount. For example, [CleverTap Leap Program](https://clevertap.com/leap/), [Early Integration Offer](https://docs.clevertap.com/docs/early-integration-offer), and so on.
* CleverTap reserves the right to modify or withdraw the offer at any time.
