CleverTap for Startups presents an exclusive offer from **[G2 Deals](https://www.g2.com/deals)**. After you utilize the free trial on the **CleverTap Essentials** Plan, you can get 75% off for the first six months. 

Check the following terms and conditions to avail this offer:

- Valid only for users currently on the CleverTap Essentials Plan (up to 100K MAUs).
- Applies only to new CleverTap accounts.
- Applicable for both the Base Plan and Add-Ons.
- Does not apply to [overages](doc:billing#overages).
- Have not received CleverTap discounts or credits before.
- Cannot be combined with any other offer, such as an annual payment discount offer, goal-based incentive, or promotional discount such as [CleverTap Leap Program](https://clevertap.com/leap/) and [Early Integration Offer](doc:early-integration-offer).
- Must be associated with G2.
