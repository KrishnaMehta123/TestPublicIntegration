CleverTap for Startups presents an offer for SVB clients. After you utilize the free trial on the **CleverTap Essentials Plan**, you can get 75% off for the first 12 months.

Check the following terms and conditions to avail of this offer:

* Applies only to users currently on the CleverTap Essentials Plan.
* Valid only for new CleverTap accounts.
* Applicable for both the Base Plan and Add-Ons.
* Does not apply to [overages](doc:billing#overages).
* Requires that the user has not previously received CleverTap discounts or credits.
* Cannot be combined with any other offer, including:  
  * Annual payment discounts
  * Goal-based incentives 
  * Promotional programs such as the [CleverTap Leap Program](https://clevertap.com/leap/) and [Early Integration Offer](doc:early-integration-offer).
* Must be associated with Silicon Valley Bank.
