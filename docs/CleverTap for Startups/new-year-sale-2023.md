This New Year, *CleverTap for Startups* offers early-stage startups a special discount. We are giving a flat 50% off on our CleverTap Basic plan giving you access to CleverTap for as low as $49 per month. 

All you have to do is follow the steps below to claim the offer:
1. Sign-up for the [CleverTap Basic Plan](https://clevertap.com/startup-pricing/).
2. Fill out the [CleverTap Leap form](https://clevertap.com/leap/) and enter *New Year* as Referrer.
3. Sit tight. You will receive an email confirmation from our end.

We will apply the offer and send you a confirmation over email within five working days of receiving your application. This email will have complete details about pricing and the applied discount before generating your first invoice. Please note that the website may still indicate the total price while we evaluate and apply the offer. 

Read the following terms and conditions to know more:
  * Offer is valid from January 1st to January 15th, 2023.
  * Offer is applicable for new accounts created during the offer period. 
  * Offer does not apply to other plans and existing customers.
  * Applicable on all MAU tiers of CleverTap Basic Plan.
  * 50% off is applicable for the first six months after the free trial period of one month.
  * Offer is applicable on the base plan and add-ons of CleverTap Basic Plan.
  * Offer is not applicable on overages.
  * Offer price indicated here is exclusive of any taxes.
  * Offer is valid for a limited period and is subject to change by CleverTap.
