# FAQs

### Q: What happens if my account does not reach 100 MAUs within 30 days?

A: If your account does not reach 100 MAUs, you will not receive the additional $250 in credits. However, the first $250 upfront credit remains applicable.

### Q: Can existing customers avail of the new customer offer?

A: No, the new customer offer is only for first-time subscribers. Existing customers can benefit from the referral program.
