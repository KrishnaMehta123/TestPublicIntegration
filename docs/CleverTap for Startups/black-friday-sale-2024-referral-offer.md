# Overview

**CleverTap for Startups** is excited to launch exclusive offers under our Black Friday Sale for 2024, especially. You can benefit from these limited-time promotions if you are an existing customer. Here is how you can make the most of it. 

# Offer Duration

**November 10, 2024 to November 30, 2024.**

# Offer Name

**Referral Program: Earn $100 for Each Successful Referral**

# Offer Eligibility

The offer is available to **all existing customers on the Essentials Plan**, regardless of their MAU tier.

# How Referral Works

Earn **$100** in credits for each new customer who signs up using your referral and clears an invoice, in whole or in part, via a payment method. Invoices paid entirely with credits do not count as invoice clearing.

The following are the key elements of the offer for existing customers:

- **Referral Form**: Submit the referral via a dedicated form present on the dashboard, with the referee's name, email, company name, and company website URL.
- **Email Notifications**: Both the referrer and referee receive an email when the referral is made and when the first invoice is paid.
- **Credits Applied**: $100 credits (INR 8,000) will be added to your account once the referee clears an invoice, in whole or in part, via a payment method.
