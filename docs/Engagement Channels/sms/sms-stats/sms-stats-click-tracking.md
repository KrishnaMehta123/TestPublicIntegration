# Overview

Once the campaign has been published, you can view the detailed campaign stats under _All Campaigns_ page. The _Stats_ tab displays the overall conversion performance and errors.

# View Campaign Stats

To view the detailed campaign stats:

1. Click **Campaigns** from the dashboard. 
2. Select your campaign and open it.

The _Stats_ tab displays the following campaign stats:

- **Sent**: Represents the total number of messages sent from CleverTap.
- **Clicked**: Displays the total number of clicks registered on the shortened link sent via CleverTap in an SMS.
- **CTR**: Represents the Click Through Rate of the SMS campaign. It is measured as (Clicked/Sent\*100).
- **Converted users**: Represents the percentage of the total recipients that performed the conversion event.

# Split of Clicks

You can track the click distribution on each link you include in your SMS campaign. If you want to send an SMS promotion for an upcoming movie on your video streaming app with multiple links for _reviews_, _promotions_, _movie merchandise_. We will record the clicks for each link in the SMS, and you can see them on the Campaign _Stats_ page.
