# Overview

Once the campaign has been published, you can view the detailed campaign stats under *All Campaigns* page. The *Stats* tab displays the overall conversion performance and errors.

# View Campaign Stats

To view the detailed campaign stats:

1. Click **Campaigns** from the dashboard. 
2. Select your campaign and open it.

The *Stats* tab displays the following campaign stats:

<Image title="SMS Stats.png" alt={2880} align="center" className="border" width="smart" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/485af51-SMS_Stats.png" />

* **Sent**: Represents the total number of messages sent from CleverTap.
* **Clicked**: Displays the total number of clicks registered on the shortened link sent via CleverTap in an SMS.
* **CTR**: Represents the Click Through Rate of the SMS campaign. It is measured as (Clicked/Sent\*100).
* **Converted users**: Represents the percentage of the total recipients that performed the conversion event.

Click **Message Trend** to view the *Performance Trend* by *Sent* and *Clicked*. You can view these trends on a daily, weekly, or monthly basis.

# Conversion performance

Click **Conversion Performance** to view *conversion*, *revenue performance*, and users' *conversion funnel*.

# Split of Clicks

You can track the click distribution on each link you include in your SMS campaign. If you want to send an SMS promotion for an upcoming movie on your video streaming app with multiple links for *reviews*, *promotions*, *movie merchandise*. We will record the clicks for each link in the SMS, and you can see them on the Campaign *Stats* page.

<Image title="Split of Clicks.png" alt={2738} align="center" className="border" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/7f791e2-Split_of_Clicks.png" />

# Errors

The **Errors** tab represents the errors that occurred while sending the SMS.
