CleverTap integrates with leading coupon and loyalty partners to help you deliver personalized offers and rewards at scale. With this feature, you can embed coupon codes generated on a partner platform directly into CleverTap campaigns and send them across channels like push notifications, emails, or In-App messages.

CleverTap currently supports the following partner platforms:

- [Loyalty Lion](doc:loyalty-lion)
- [Talon.One](doc:talonone)
- [Voucherify](doc:voucherify)
- [Yotpo](doc:yotpo)
