We have observed some issues while installing the CleverTap app on your Shopify store. We recommend you uninstall the CleverTap app from your Shopify admin dashboard and try installing it again.

To uninstall the CleverTap app from the Shopify admin dashboard, visit this URL - https\://\<**_your-shopify-store-name_**>.myshopify.com/admin/settings/apps for your store and click _Remove_.
