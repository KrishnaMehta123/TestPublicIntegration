# Overview

[LeadSquared](https://www.leadsquared.com) is a sales execution and marketing automation platform that helps businesses manage leads and drive sales efficiently.

Integrating CleverTap with LeadSquared enables real-time, two-way lead syncing. You can capture leads from CleverTap into LeadSquared and transfer enriched lead profiles back to CleverTap, helping automate campaigns and personalize user engagement. By integrating LeadSquared with CleverTap, you can:

* Sync lead activity from CleverTap to LeadSquared to automate sales workflows.
* Push qualified leads from LeadSquared to CleverTap to trigger personalized journeys.
* Maintain consistent lead data across both platforms to power real-time engagement.

# Prerequisites for Integration

The following are the prerequisites for LeadSquared and CleverTap integration:

* Ensure you have a CleverTap account with administrative access
* Ensure you have access to your active [LeadSquared account](https://www.leadsquared.com/signup/).
