CleverTap supports sending SMS through Netcore. 

Sign up with Netcore and then follow the steps below in the CleverTap Dashboard for a successful integration.
  * In the CleverTap Dashboard, navigate to Settings and click on Integrate SMS.
  * Select Netcore.
  * In the Username text box, enter your Netcore username.
  * In the Password text box, enter your Netcore password.
  * In the Feed Id text box, enter the Feed Id provided by Netcore.
