# Overview

The _Today_ dashboard gives you a snapshot and trend analysis of your active and engaged users and their activities in your application across your apps and website.

To get to this view, navigate to _Dashboard_ > _Today_.

This view shows the following:

 _Users Activity_ represents users that have performed any activity on the app or website. You can also view the number of users who performed any activity on the application or website yesterday and a week ago.

- _New Users_ represent first-time users in your app or website on that day.
- _Returning Users_ represent users returning to your app or website on that day.
