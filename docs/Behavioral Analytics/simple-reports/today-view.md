# Overview

The *Today* dashboard gives you a snapshot and trend analysis of your active and engaged users and their activities in your application across your apps and website.

<Image title="Today Dashboard" alt={2386} align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/bdbb399-Today_board.png">
  Today Dashboard
</Image>

To get to this view, navigate to *Dashboard* > *Today*.

This view shows the following:

 *Users Activity* represents users that have performed any activity on the app or website. You can also view the number of users who performed any activity on the application or website yesterday and a week ago.

<Image title="User Activity - Today Board" alt={2378} align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/14ddac2-User_Activity_-_Today_Board.png">
  User Activity Analytics
</Image>

* *New Users* represent first-time users in your app or website on that day.
* *Returning Users* represent users returning to your app or website on that day.

<Image title="New vs Returning - Today Board" alt={2252} align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/a3caa80-New_vs._Returning_-_Today_Board.png">
  New vs Returning Users Analytics
</Image>

* *Usage Source* shows the split of total users on a select day by platform.

<Image title="Today Board Usage Source" alt={585} align="center" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/2992656-Today_board_usage_source.png">
  Usage Source Data
</Image>

In addition, the *Activity Stream* dashboard shows some of the users who are active in your app and the events that they are undertaking. Navigate to *Boards* > *Today* > *Activity Stream* tab.
