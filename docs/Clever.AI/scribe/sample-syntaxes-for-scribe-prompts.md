The following sample prompts can help you get started with Scribe quickly. You can experiment with more prompts to find a more suitable message tone and style.

# Syntax 1: Topic: \[next line] Language: \[next line] Font: \[next line] Style: \[prompt]

Use a combination of multiple prompts to achieve a more specific outcome. You can specify the topic, language, mix of languages, language font, style, etc. 

* Topic: Specify the topic of the message
* Brand Name: Specify the name of the brand.
* Language: Specify a single language, a mix of two or more languages, Hinglish, etc. Example: For Hinglish, specify the prompt as a mix of Hindi and English words.
* Font: Specify the font, such as Devanagari, English, Tamil, etc. 
* Style: You can specify Bollywood jingles, weather reporting, rap songs, clickbait, etc.
* Include Words: Specify a specific word to be included in the message. 
* Exclude Words: Specify a specific word to be excluded from the message.
